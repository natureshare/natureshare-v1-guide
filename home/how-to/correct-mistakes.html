Correcting Mistakes

NatureShare deliberately makes it hard to delete masses of information quickly to stop people doing so maliciously. But you can rectify all the mistakes you make.

In your own observations, if you are logged in, there are icons that allow you to delete a photo, edit date & time, edit location coordinates (or click on 'show large map' and change location via the map) or edit the description you entered. These icons are not there in observations that are not yours.

At the bottom of all observation pages and at the bottom of all species pages there is a link called "Show delete links". Click on this and you can delete/change:
 On the Observations Page
  On the Species Page
 Species Names
 Tags
  Common Names,  Classifications
 Categories,  Attributes
 Associations

In addition to this you can also:
  • Remove an observation from a collection (eg. if you added an observation to a collection by mistake) - you can do this by:
    • note the date & time of your observation
    • go to the collection
    • click on 'observations'
    • find the page that contains the errant observation (observations are listed by date)
    • click on 'Show delete links'
    • delete your observation
