How to Logout

First, get to the home page (eg. by clicking on the logo - see picture right)
... then add  /logout  to the web address; ie.

natureshare.org.au/logout/
or
http://natureshare.org.au/logout/

Or click on the link below to logout from here:
natureshare.org.au/logout/
