Link to Collections

Every 'Collection' has its own web address, and that web address never changes. This means you can:


  • Link directly to a collection - instead of having a webmaster continually updating a website every time a new species, photo or species information is added to a collection, this is done automatically and immediately each time a person adds to the NatureShare collection. For example, here is a link from this website to the Butterflies of Riddells Creek collection and the Native Plants of the Victorian Volcanic Plains, both of which are being continually added to and therefore are being continually updated by users of NatureShare.

  • Link to a range of collections for a local area - here is an example of a live website that links to a range of flora and fauna collections in a local area. NB. your links can be a any collection on NatureShare, not just those you administer.

If you find other ways to link NatureShare 'collections' into what you do, let us know, so we can share it with others.
