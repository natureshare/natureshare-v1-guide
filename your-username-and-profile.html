Your Username & Profile

If you haven't changed you Username, please do so now!

After you login for the first time, the next step is to set up your user page (known as your 'Contributor' page). From the moment you first login you are allocated a User number and you will be known as User#28 (whatever number we are at) until you change it - NB. User#28 will then appear as your name on all your Observations until you change it.

Setting up Your Username & Profile is Easy:
  • Step 1 - When logged in, click on the 'User#28' text in the main/side menu.
  • Step 2 - Click on 'Edit name and/or email'. Add your name (eg. "Francis White") and add your email address (note that your email address is kept private but if there is a problem NatureShare can contact you).
  • Step 3 - Click on 'Edit your profile description' and add some text about yourself.

The following tabs appear on your 'Contributor' page:
  • Summary
    This is a summary of your details. Only your name (which automatically appears on all your observations) and your 'profile description' is accessible by others.

  • Observations
    Here you can view all your observations.

  • Collections
    Here is a list of all the Collections you are a member of.

  • Log
    This lists all your activity on NatureShare ... everything!
